Avoiding Freshmen 15

Image
1. Get enough sleep.

2. Don't skip breakfast.

3. Walk off your stress.

4. Don't study in an area with food. Try to study in a place that prohibits food.

5. Include fruits and vegetables in all of your meals.

6. Don't drink your calories.

7. Keep healthy snacks and beverages in your dorm room.

8. DON'T look on the internet for nutritional guidance.

9. Snack around the clock. It can help avoid bingeing.

10. Stay in shape, you can go to the gym with a friend or two.

11. Join a sports club.

12. Try your best to manage your stress, if you are stressed out it can cause you to eat more than you want to.

13. Don't multitask while you eat.

Studying

Image
1. Look over your notes to make sure you undertand it.

2. Make friends in your class so you can ask questions when you need to.

3. When note taking, make sure you record the key points of a lecture and don't try to write down everything the teacher says.This can help you get good grades.

4. Stay organized: Keep a detailed calendar and organize your class materials.

5. Unplug and Reconnect: Limit the time you spend on the internet.

6. Don't put off studying and cram. Leanr to pace yourself when you study.

7. Don't over study, learn to manage your time. (Time Management is essential).

8. Find your zone: Find the best environment for you to study and it can be a quiet place or your can listen to music. It is what you prefer.

9. Make sure that you take a break, it can help you stay organized and it makes sure that you are at your peak for mental health.

10. Track everything in your planner or journal. It can help you stay on top of your world.

11. Create a routine and get comfortable with it.

12. Don't let a bad grade, keep you down.
